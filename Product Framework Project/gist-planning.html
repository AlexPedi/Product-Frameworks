GIST Planning

By Itamar Gilad
HackerNoon.com

Developed during Itamar's experience as a PM at Google, GIST Planning incorporates principles from Lean Startup and Agile Development. Using the building blocks of Goals, Ideas, Step projects, and Tasks allow for flexible planning, reduced overhead, and stronger alignment compared to traditional product roadmaps.
